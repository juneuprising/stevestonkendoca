The Mecca of Kendo
in Canada

Founded in 1914 as Yokikan Dojo, the Steveston Kendo Club is one of the first kendo dojos in Canada. It closed in 1939 due to World War II and reopened after the war. Today, it operates at the Steveston Martial Arts Centre and has over 60 active members annually.

Kendo from many
walks of life

Steveston Kendo Club welcomes kendoka of all ages, from as young as 8 to over 60. While many members are from Richmond, others travel from across the Lower Mainland to train. Kendo offers a unique physical activity that helps develop discipline, confidence, and spirit.

Beginners Course

Our introductory summer kendo course welcomes beginners, helping them build a strong foundation in both the physical and philosophical aspects of the art. It's also a great short-term experience to see if kendo is right for you.
