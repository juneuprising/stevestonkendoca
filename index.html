A kendo club rooted in history

Founded in 1914, the Steveston Kendo Club is one of the oldest kendo clubs in Canada. Originally founded under the name Yokikan, it closed its doors in 1939 during World War II. After the war, Steveston Kendo Club reopened its doors and is now home to over 60 active members. The Club is located in the heart of Steveston, at the iconic Steveston Martial Arts Centre.

Kendo for all
walks of life

The Steveston Kendo Club hosts kendo practices to members ages 8 and over. Members come from all over the lower mainland in BC, from a diverse range of fitness levels, cultures, and learning goals.

Beginners course

The summer introductory kendo course is designed to welcome beginners and build a solid foundation in both the physical and philosophical aspects of the art as well as to provide a short term experience to see if kendo is right for them.
