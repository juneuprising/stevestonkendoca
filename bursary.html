About The
SKC Bursary

Every year, the Steveston Kendo Club Bursary is given to high school graduates who have shown a commitment to both their community and the art of kendo.

The bursary, which is available to active club members pursuing post-secondary study, honors individuals who uphold the fundamental kendo ideals of discipline, tenacity, and respect for one another.

The bursary seeks to support young kendoka in maintaining their ties to the martial arts tradition while pursuing their academic and personal development.

Requirements

Be a Steveston Kendo Club Member for at least 2 years
Proof of high school Transcript
Proof of Post Secondary Enrollment
Have the Discretion of the Sensei Cadre
